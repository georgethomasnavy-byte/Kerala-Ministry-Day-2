P. K. Kunhalikutty

Position

Minister for Industries, Commerce, Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence

Description

P. K. Kunhalikutty is a senior leader of the Indian Union Muslim League (IUML) and serves as the Minister for Industries, Commerce, Information Technology, and Artificial Intelligence. He is responsible for promoting industrial growth, attracting investments, and advancing Kerala's technology sector.

Party

Indian Union Muslim League (IUML)

Constituency

Vengara

Experience

One of Kerala's most experienced politicians, P. K. Kunhalikutty has served multiple terms as Minister and Member of the Legislative Assembly. He has played a major role in shaping industrial, educational, and developmental policies in the state.
