A. P. Anil Kumar

Position

Minister for Revenue

Description

A. P. Anil Kumar serves as the Minister for Revenue in the Kerala Government. He is responsible for land administration, revenue collection, disaster management, land records, and matters related to government land across the state.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Wandoor

Experience

A. P. Anil Kumar is a senior Congress leader who has represented Wandoor multiple times in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He has previously served as a minister and is known for his work in public administration and rural development.
