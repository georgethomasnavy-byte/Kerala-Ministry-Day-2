V. D. Satheesan

Position

Chief Minister of Kerala

Description

V. D. Satheesan is the Chief Minister of Kerala and leader of the United Democratic Front (UDF) government. He is responsible for guiding the state's administration and overseeing important departments such as Finance, Law, and General Administration.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Paravur

Experience

A senior Congress leader, V. D. Satheesan has served multiple terms in the Kerala Legislative Assembly and is known for his role in state politics and governance.
