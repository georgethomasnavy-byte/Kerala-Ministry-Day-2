K. Muraleedharan

Position

Minister for Health, Medical Education, Devaswom and Food Safety

Description

K. Muraleedharan serves as the Minister for Health, Medical Education, Devaswom and Food Safety. He is responsible for strengthening Kerala's healthcare system, medical institutions, public health programs, temple administration under Devaswom boards, and food safety regulations.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Vattiyoorkavu

Experience

K. Muraleedharan is a veteran Congress leader and former Member of Parliament. He has held several important positions in Kerala politics and is known for his long public service career and active involvement in state development.
