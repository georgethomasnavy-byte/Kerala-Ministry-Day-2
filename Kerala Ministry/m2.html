Ramesh Chennithala

Position

Minister for Home and Vigilance

Description

Ramesh Chennithala is one of Kerala's most experienced politicians and serves as the Minister for Home and Vigilance. He is responsible for law enforcement, public safety, police administration, and anti-corruption oversight in the state.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Haripad

Experience

A veteran Congress leader, Ramesh Chennithala has served as Opposition Leader, KPCC President, Minister, and Member of Parliament. He has been a prominent figure in Kerala politics for several decades.
