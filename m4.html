Sunny Joseph

Position

Minister for Electricity, Environment and Parliamentary Affairs

Description

Sunny Joseph serves as the Minister for Electricity, Environment and Parliamentary Affairs. He is responsible for Kerala's power sector, environmental protection initiatives, and coordination of government business in the Legislative Assembly.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Peravoor

Experience

Sunny Joseph is a senior Congress leader and experienced legislator who has represented Peravoor multiple times in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He has been actively involved in public service, environmental issues, and legislative affairs.
