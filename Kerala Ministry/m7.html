Roji M. John

Position

Minister for Higher Education and Technical Education

Description

Roji M. John serves as the Minister for Higher Education and Technical Education in the Kerala Government. He is responsible for universities, colleges, technical institutions, academic reforms, research initiatives, and skill-oriented higher education programs across the state.

Party

Indian National Congress (INC)

Constituency

Angamaly

Experience

Roji M. John is a prominent Congress leader from Ernakulam district and has represented Angamaly in the Kerala Legislative Assembly. He is known for his active participation in youth politics, education-related initiatives, and constituency development activities.
